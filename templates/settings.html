Font Settings

Choose your preferred font type to enhance readability. The OpenDyslexic font is specifically designed to help readers with dyslexia, while Roboto offers a clean, modern look optimised for screen readability.

About the Fonts

OpenDyslexic is a typeface designed to mitigate some of the common reading errors caused by dyslexia. The letters have heavy weighted bottoms to indicate direction, unique letter shapes to prevent confusion, and consistent letter spacing to help improve readability.

Roboto is a modern sans-serif typeface designed for digital readability. Its clean lines and geometric shapes make it ideal for screens and long-form reading.
